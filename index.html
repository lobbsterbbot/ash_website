The Approach

I work with a simple framework: clarity first, then execution. Most founders jump straight to execution. That's where everything falls apart.

Audit Your Landscape

Map your competitive position, identify market gaps, and understand where partnerships create real leverage.

Define Your Strategy

Get crystal clear on which partnerships matter, why they matter, and what you need to make them work.

Identify & Connect

Build a target list and create warm introduction paths. No cold outreach. Strategic, thoughtful approach.

Execute & Measure

Structure deals, negotiate terms, and build accountability. Then track what actually moves the needle.
