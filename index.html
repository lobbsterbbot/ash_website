About

I've been on both sides.

I've seen partnerships from the inside — what kills deals and what makes them stick. I've worked with founders at the moment they're trying to figure out their go-to-market, and I know exactly what that pressure feels like.

Most early-stage founders have good instincts but limited networks and bandwidth. Partnerships are often a nice-to-have in their head, but they're actually a critical lever for growth. My job is to help you see where partnerships fit in your story, and then execute on them.

I work best with founders who are past the "pure product" phase and starting to think about distribution. You don't need venture-scale ambitions — just the hunger to build something real and the willingness to do the work.
